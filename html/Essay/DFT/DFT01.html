How did I get involved with DFT?

When I joined the Yang Lab, Prof. Yang encouraged me to cultivate multidisciplinary mindset and benefit from my undergraduate background in computer science.

Given the Yang Lab's focus on zwitterionic biomedical hydrogels, my future research may benefit from incorporating DFT, molecular dynamics, or machine learning techniques.

What can DFT do? What is DFT?

When encountering something entirely new, I tend to first explore what it can do and how it might benefit me, before delving into what it actually is.

1.Transition States: Calculate reaction process is a primary application of DFT, but it not relevant to my research needs.

2.Potential Energy Surface Scanning: Determine the steady-state configuration and geometric parameter changes of molecules, I don't need it.

3.Spectral Prediction: Predicting electronic transition energy levels and corresponding absorption spectra by molecular structure, I also don't need it.

4.Intermolecular Interactions: Calculate the interactions between molecules.
