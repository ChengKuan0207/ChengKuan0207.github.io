What is DFT?

While Wikipedia provides a rigorous explanation of DFT, my limited knowledge of quantum mechanics and quantum chemistry makes it challenging to fully grasp at first.

Prior to explaining my perspective on DFT, a brief overview of key concepts is required.

As we all know, Newton's second law perfectly describes motion in our everyday world.

When we zoom in to the quantum scale, we find that the Schrödinger equation becomes the fundamental equation of motion.

The Schrödinger equation describes the probability distribution of a particle in space through its wave function.
